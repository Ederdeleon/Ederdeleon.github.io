LibKey Nomad Table Tent

Brigham Young University-Idaho has access to online magazines and libraries that they wanted to advertise around campus with a 3-sided poster on tables. I designed the content and the poster that was distributed around the entire campus. I will share some drafts I did and some of the feedback I got from my coworkers.
